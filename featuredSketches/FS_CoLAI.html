Featured sketcher

This season's featured sketchers are the students of the City of London Academy - Islington

sketchPatch took part in this year's CoLA-I ArtsFest, an annual festival at City of London Academy Islington in association with the Barbican. The festival provides an opportunity for the pupils of CoLA-I and a range of professional artists to work together on projects from dance, poetry and literature, filmmaking, photography and computer art. sketchPatch delivered creative programming workshops to over 200 pupils throughout one week, which resulted in some amazing and beautiful computer art.

A big thank you to year 7, 8 & 9 at CoLA-I for making our week amazing!
